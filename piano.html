Piano Lessons

What styles of piano do you teach?‍

We teach all styles, from rock, pop and jazz to country, blues and classical.

How qualified are Irving's piano instructors?

Our faculty have received their musical education from such prestigious institutions as Julliard, the Berklee College of Music, Texas University, UNT, and SMU piano performance and pedagogy programs. Many have toured the US and Europe, and bring over 25 years of professional performance and teaching experience to your lessons.

Overview


Whether you aspire to play Bach or Alicia Keys, Irving Fine Arts Academy dedicated piano instructors will help you become the pianist you want to be. Our faculty will introduce you to the fundamentals of sight reading, technique and theory, and incorporate your favorite songs into your lessons. You do not need a piano at home to take lessons; a simple full-size electronic keyboard will suffice for the first six months.


What’s the #1 reason to study the piano at the Academy?

Our piano instructors are warm and friendly, and get to know each student and parent on a personalF level. We work with each individual or parent to craft a lesson plan that fits the student’s unique needs and favorite type of music. Whether you're playing in local bands, a seven year old taking their first lesson, or a fifty year old returning to the piano after many years, our teachers will share their love for the piano and help you set goals to achieve your musical aspirations.

What will I learn from piano lessons at the Academy?

Whether you want to learn Bach or Alicia Keys, our teachers are dedicated to helping you become the pianist you want to be. They will show you the fundamentals of sight reading, technique and theory, and incorporate your favorite songs into your lessons.


Do I have to know how to read music?

No. Beginners are welcome!



Do I need a piano to learn the instrument?

No, you do not need a piano at home to take lessons. A simple full size electronic keyboard will suffice for the first 6 months. We offer a variety of keyboards recommendations for beginners, some starting as low as $199.

Why Choose Us



COMMITTED TO EXCELLENCE

Customized lesson plans to fit all ages and genres of music. Special needs children thrive in a loving environment built to foster their musical goals and abilities. Celebrate progress through Book Graduation Recitals, Trophies, Certificates and the Musical Ladder Wristband System ™.


FAMILY FRIENDLY

With over 4,000 square feet, our 14 practice rooms which includes a large dance/theater hall, we have the most spacious and parent-friendly waiting area in Irving with comfortable chairs, toys, WI-FI, filtered water service and large observation windows on every door. Our state-of-the-art spacious practice rooms include top of the line upright or grand pianos.


MUSIC RECITALS

Students are given the opportunity to perform in 5 recitals including a yearly concert on a 9ft Steinway in a world-class performance hall.


DANCE LESSONS

Our award-winning faculty, and beautiful new professional Dura-Dance sprung flooring will create the perfect environment to nurture dancers from toddlers to adult. IFAA offers a dance style for everyone, from classical ballet to jazz and hip-hop. Let the joy of classical ballet begin for your child as we introduce them to ballet fundamentals including body alignment, basic ballet technique, terminology, and musicality. Self-esteem, discipline, and confidence will be nurtured in every student to allow them to flourish to reach their fullest potential as artists.


MONTHLY THEORY / PERFORMANCE CLASSES INCLUDED

Miss a class? Using the Texas Music Theory Exam, students enrolled in PRIVATE lessons are encouraged to attend 60-minute music theory/performance class monthly. Taking the exam is optional. p>

CUSTOMIZED LESSONS

Parents are encouraged to attend the lesson with their child until the child is sight-reading independently. The parent participation allows the teacher to communicate, as well as allow the parent to record a short segment of the “main point” at the end of each lesson for the child to watch and use during their practice at home. Parents say the children love watching and practicing to these videos so much that the child asks for them to be played just so they can hear the teacher talking just to THEM!


SUZUKI PROGRAM

The Suzuki method is the perfect foundation for all styles of music, as the child gets a firm classical foundation based in technique, ear-training and firm mastery of their instrument through the finest classical music in the world. Half the price of other similar programs in the area, we make quality music lessons affordable for everyone.
