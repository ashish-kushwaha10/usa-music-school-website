About Irving Ballet School

Our Studio

Nurture the beat in your feet! Our award winning faculty, and beautiful new professional Dura-Dance sprung flooring will create the perfect environment to nurture dancers from ages 3 to adult. Irving Ballet School offers a dance style for everyone, from classical ballet to salsa and family style ballroom. Let the joy of classical ballet begin for your child as we introduce them to ballet fundamentals including body alignment, basic ballet technique, terminology and musicality. Self esteem, discipline and confidence will be nurtured in every student to allow them to flourish to reach their fullest potential as artists.

We believe


strength

Our spirits are uplifted and strengthened when we move our bodies. We are born naturally rythmic from the first beat of our heart. It is up to us to nurture and strengthen our rythm and our musical souls. Dance, movement from the beginning of time has been used to express joy and hope around the world. Irving Ballet works to use movement and dance to unite our community and our world.


Discipline

Through support, nurturing and encouragement, the fine art of classical ballet and other dance styles is nutured in a loving environment. All shapes, all ages, all levels. We grow stronger together.


Integrity

We strive to nurture the dancers in the highest form of Classical Ballet formation, taking time and not rushing students through. With our stellar faculty and high standards, we strive to build a solid company of dancers who encourage and inspire each other to reach their fullest potential as artists.
