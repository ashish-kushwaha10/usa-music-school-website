is Irving #1 choice for music & dance lessons

Experience the Benefits of Becoming an Irving Fine Arts Academy Student


While you are here researching the best music and dance school, please consider the benefits of becoming an IFAA student and find out why we are Irving's number one choice for music and dance lessons.

Full Service Music Education

Serving the city of Irving for 9 years, providing excellence in music education,with 1st place winners for five consecutive years at Irving, Texas premiere teen talent competition, Irving’s Got Talent. We are the only full service music school in Irving, Texas that offers music lessons in Piano, Guitar, Violin, Drums, Voice, Cello, Banjo, Mandolin, and Ukulele all in ONE location! Conveniently located directly at the corner of Beltline and Story, across the street from Great Hearts charter school. Easy access for students coming from Irving, Dallas, Coppell, Grapevine, Hurst, Euless and Bedford.
Our goal is to inspire students of all ages with a lifelong love of music. Our hope is to impact lives and our community through music and arts with our dedicated world-class faculty.

Ballet and Hip hop


Nurture the beat in your feet! Our award winning faculty, and beautiful new professional Dura-Dance sprung flooring will create the perfect environment to nurture dancers from ages 3 to adult. IFAA offers a dance style for everyone, from classical ballet to salsa and family style ballroom. Let the joy of classical ballet begin for your child as we introduce them to ballet fundamentals including body alignment, basic ballet technique, terminology and musicality. Self esteem, discipline and confidence will be nurtured in every student to allow them to flourish to reach their fullest potential as artists.

Professional Musical Theatre

Calling all actors, singer and dancers! Our nationally recognized director director will help bring out the confidence and stage presence of even the shyest speakers. From beginners to experienced actors, joining the cast of an MTI professional musical production is the perfect experience to grow in speaking confidence, vocal dynamics and stage presence. Our first production sold out 4 shows in 48 hours! Let the acting begin!

Committed to Excellence


Customized lesson plans to fit all ages and genres of music. Students of all levels thrive in a loving environment built to foster their musical goals and abilities. Hand-selected world class faculty with years of professional experience as well as graduates from top universities such as Julliard and Eastman School of Music.

Family Friendly

With over 4,000 square feet, we have the most spacious and parent friendly waiting area in Irving, Texas. Instead of waiting in a music retail showroom, our clients enjoy spacious practice rooms to sit with their child in the lesson, or wait in the parent’s lounge with comfortable chairs, toys WI-FI and bottled water service. We also provide large observation windows on every door. Our state of the art spacious practice rooms include top of the line upright or grand pianos.

Music Recitals


Students are given the opportunity to perform each year on a grand piano in a worldclass performance hall. A fun event for the whole family, these recitals celebrate all our students hard work as they allow hard earned practice benchmarks to be recognized and celebrated publicly with certificates and trophies.

Suzuki Program

The Suzuki method is the perfect foundation for all styles of music, as the child gets a strong classical foundation based in technique, ear-training and firm mastery of their instrument through the finest classical music in the world. Students work toward graduation completion ceremonies at the end of each Suzuki Book, which includes a special recital and a trophy. Find out more about Suzuki and our Suzuki program director, Zoe Madewell (The Muzik Mom)

The Most Qualified Teaching Faculty


Our teachers all have either college training or professional experience. Our music teachers are dedicated to learning about your personal goals and helping you to achieve them.

Wide Range of Lesson Times, Seven Days a Week

We are the only full service music school in Irving, Texas that offers music lessons in Piano, Guitar, Violin, Drums, Voice, Cello, Banjo, Mandolin, and Ukulele, as well as group classes in EARLY CHILDHOOD MUSIC, DRAMA and ADULT KEYBOARD. To accommodate your busy schedule classes are offered from early morning to late at night. Convenient morning hours for home schoolers, seniors and busy professionals. 9:00am-9:00pm Monday-Friday 9:00am-3:00pm Saturday 1:00pm-5:00pm Sunday

Proven Track Record of Excellence since 2007


We have a proven track record of excellence, offering lessons to some of Irving, Texas finest families for almost ten years. From winning local talent competitions, to just having a blast performing for friends and family, our students learn to truly love music. Our annual fall Costume Play-In allows children to perform their latest piece dressed in their favorite costume. This recital has become one of our biggest hits as parents enjoy the opportunity to grab a picture of their favorite Batman, Ninja or Disney Princess playing their instrument for a treat!

Trophies, Certificates and Wristbands to Celebrate Achievement

Celebrate progress through Book Graduation Recitals, Trophies, Certificates and the Musical Ladder Wristband System. Benchmarks and goals make the discipline it takes to master an instrument, fun and exciting!

Month to Month Lessons


We do not require semester minimums for lessons. Every student is here on a month to month basis. If you are ready to start lessons in Piano, Guitar, Violin, Drums, Voice, Cello, Banjo, Mandolin or Ukulele you have nothing to lose as we will not commit you to a long term contract.CONTACT US NOW TO RESERVE YOUR SLOT.



It's easy to take the first step

Ready to start? You have nothing to lose.

Sign up is easy and parent-friendly, with helpful staff at the front desk seven days a week to serve you. Because we only offer lessons on a month-to-month basis, there are no large upfront payments, no complicated contracts, no annual material or performance fees, and no semester minimums.
