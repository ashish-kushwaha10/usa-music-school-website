We are an Official Family Safety Plan Music School!

In order to keep our students, staff, and teachers safe, we have implemented the Family Safety Plan in our schools! This includes glass windows on lesson rooms to foster transparency between teachers and parents, full-time office staff so that students and teachers are never left unsupervised, requiring extensive background checks of all of our teachers and staff, security cameras in all rooms, and an open-door policy for parents, which means that they’re always invited to sit in on their child’s lessons. WHY CHOOSE US?

STATE OF THE ART MODERN FACILITY
Our spacious practice rooms offer nicely kept pianos, viewing widows, dura dance sprung flooring, and an in-house performance hall.
PERFORMANCE OPPORTUNITIES
Our students participate with their peers in 8+ recitals each year, including a Spring Recital in a multi-million dollar concert auditorium. Each recital, included in the tuition, showcases each student’s progress and achievement in their area of choice (music, dance, drama). Our in-house recitals create a great opportunity for students to build charisma and confidence with their peers and audience.
SUZUKI PROGRAM
Irving Fine Arts Academy offers private and group lessons for students, as young as 2, using the internationally renowned Suzuki Program. Each student, and their choice instrument, will have a custom path that blends a variety of methods and styles to suit the understanding for each student and reach their potential and joy in learning music and the arts.
AFFORDABLE GROUP CLASSES
We offer safe, small group classes that encourage harmony within peers. Our non-profit, The Finding Mozart Project, offers aid and a path for every individual to learn the arts.
DRAMA AND MUSICAL THEATER PROGRAM
We offer professional, well-known Broadway and Disney MTI musical productions each year around the holiday season. Students engage in acting, singing, and dancing within a compartmentalized schedule that builds strength for each individual in the assembly.
BALLET AND HIP HOP DANCE
Our award winning faculty, and beautiful professional Dura-Dance sprung flooring will create the perfect environment to nurture dancers from ages 3 to adult. We focus on encouraging fundamentals, basic ballet technique, terminology and musicality to reflect positively in self-esteem, disciple and confidence for every one of our dancers.
